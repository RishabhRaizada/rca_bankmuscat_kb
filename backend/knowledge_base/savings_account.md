# Savings Account & Savings Plan — Knowledge Base

## Overview
Bank Muscat offers multiple savings products for different demographics and goals. This category covers savings account inquiries, interest issues, account opening, and the Themaar systematic savings plan.

## Products

### Savings Accounts
- **Al Mazyona Savings Account**: Prize draw savings scheme. Eligible for OMR and AED. Regular prize draws with cash prizes. Interest-free but prize draw eligible.
- **Interest Bearing Savings Account**: Standard interest-earning savings. Available in OMR, AED, USD, GBP, EUR, SAR, KWD, BHD, QAR.
- **Interest Free Account (Non Al Mazyona)**: No interest, no prize draw. Available in OMR, AED, USD, GBP, SAR, KWD, BHD, QAR.

### Specialised Accounts
- **Children's Account (Under 14)**: No minimum balance fee if balance falls below RO 50. Parents can view via mBanking. Includes free insurance if RO 50 maintained.
- **Floosi Account (Ages 13–17)**: Debit card with daily limit RO 50, monthly RO 200. No minimum balance fees.
- **Shababi Youth Account (Ages 18–22/23)**: Minimum balance RO 10. Free branded debit card, exclusive youth discounts, contactless wristband available (one-time RO 5 fee).

### Savings Plans
- **Themaar Savings Plan**: Systematic savings plan. RO 10–1,000 per month. 1–10 year tenure. Fixed competitive interest for full term. Free life insurance coverage up to RO 50,000. No penalties for missed monthly deposits.
- **Themaar Retirement**: Variant targeting a specific monthly pension. Customer selects retirement age and target amount.

## Online Account Opening (via mBanking)
- Available for eligible Omani residents with valid national ID.
- Non-residents, FATCA customers, expired IDs, un-updated KYC → must visit branch.
- Process: Requests → Accounts → Instant Savings Account Opening → select type, currency, branch → OTP → instant activation.
- Newly opened account: customer must log out and log back in to see it.

## Common Issues & Root Causes

### Savings Plan Not Cancelled / Savings Plan Requested but Not Created
- **Root Cause**: Human Error (instruction not actioned in T24), System Error (auto-processing failure).
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer; Rectify in T24.

### Monthly Interest / Profit Not Added Back
- **What it is**: Expected monthly interest credit did not appear in account.
- **Root Cause**: Human Error or System Error.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer; Rectify; verify interest accrual in T24.

### Incorrect Rate / Interest Not Paid / Compound Interest Not Paid
- **Root Cause**: Human Error (wrong rate applied at opening/renewal), System Error.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer; Rectify; recalculate and credit correct amount.

### Account Not Opened (Branch)
- **Root Cause**: Incomplete Documents, Invalid Documents, Operational Issue, Staff Delay, System Error.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer For Missing Documents; Open Account; Rectify.

### Standing Order Not Executed / Created / Closed
- **What it is**: Customer's recurring transfer instruction (standing order) was not processed, not set up, or not cancelled as requested.
- **Root Cause**: Human Error or System Error.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer; Rectify in T24.

### Enquiry on Online Account Opening
- **Known pattern**: Customers are often unaware that savings accounts can be opened via mBanking. This is a communication/visibility gap.
- **Root Cause**: Operational Issue — insufficient promotion of digital account opening.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer And Inform Accordingly; walk through online process steps.

## Key Facts
- Standing instructions for scheduled/recurring transfers: RO 0.500 bz charge per transaction (vs. free for manual transfers within Bank Muscat).
- Interest is credited based on the frequency chosen at account opening (monthly, quarterly, etc.).
- Al Mazyona draws are periodic and random — customers cannot predict when they will win.
