# Bank Muscat Internal Systems & Terminology Glossary

## Core Banking Systems
- **T24 (Temenos T24)**: Primary core banking system. Handles all account management, transaction records, loan processing, interest calculations, and customer data. "Check T24" = look up the transaction/account in the core banking system.
- **Prime (Misys/Finastra Prime)**: Secondary system used for card transactions, international payments, and dispute management. Used alongside T24. "Check the transaction in Prime" = verify card or payment transaction records.
- **Additional Services Application**: Internal portal used to process customer service requests (phone updates, address changes, email updates, ID updates). "Check Additional Services Application" = review the pending/completed service request in this portal.

## Self-Service Machines
- **ATM**: Cash withdrawal machines. Bank Muscat ATMs, GCC bank ATMs, Meethaq ATMs.
- **CDM (Cash Deposit Machine)**: Cash deposit machines at Bank Muscat locations. Deposits should credit the account immediately or within the business day.
- **BCDM (Bank Muscat CDM)**: Bank Muscat-branded CDM. "BCDM Location" = identifying which CDM the customer used.
- **Machine Roll**: Physical cash cassette log inside ATM/CDM. "Machine Roll" as an action = check the physical cash reconciliation record to verify dispensed/deposited amounts.
- **ATM Details / CDM Details / FT Number**: Transaction reference numbers and logs from the specific machine — used for dispute investigation.

## Authentication & Security
- **Soft Token**: Bank Muscat mobile app for two-factor authentication. Generates time-based OTPs. Common issue: shows "application under maintenance" error. Resolution: advise reinstall and re-registration.
- **Secure Token**: Physical hardware device generating OTPs for internet banking. Issued at branch.
- **OTP (One-Time Password)**: Time-sensitive authentication code sent via SMS or generated by token device.
- **Corporate Online / Corp-Online**: Internet banking portal for corporate customers.

## Documents & Certificates
- **NOC (No Objection Certificate)**: Certificate confirming the bank has no objection to a customer's request (e.g., selling a financed property). SLA: 5 working days maximum.
- **CLC (Clearance Letter Certificate)**: Certificate confirming full settlement of a loan/finance. SLA: 5 working days maximum.
- **AOF (Account Opening Form)**: Form customers fill at branch to open accounts. "Check Customer AOF Form" = verify the original application to check what the customer submitted.
- **Mulkiya (Title Deed)**: Property ownership document. Held by bank during home finance, released after full settlement.
- **Krooki**: Site plan/sketch of property, held by bank during home finance.

## Internal Departments
- **Back Office**: Internal operations team processing loan/finance applications, document verification, NOC/CLC issuance, and settlement. Not customer-facing. Delays here affect loan-related complaints.
- **CAD (Credit Administration Department)**: Handles credit/finance administration, collateral management, rate verifications, and settlement processing. "Ask CAD Team" = escalate rate or credit admin queries to this department.
- **ARM (Account Relationship Manager)**: Relationship manager role. Handles high-value customer accounts, securities information, and balance confirmation requests.
- **Card Services**: Department responsible for card production, personalisation, and readying for delivery.
- **Mailing Department**: Handles physical card and document delivery from Card Services to branches.
- **E-Channels Team**: Responsible for ATM/CDM network operations and uptime.
- **IT Team / IT Teams**: Internal technology team. Escalate system errors, technical issues, archived statements.
- **Account Operation Team**: Handles e-statement ID issues and account-level operational problems.
- **Bancassurance Business Team**: Handles insurance-linked banking products and blocked amounts related to insurance.

## Regulatory & Compliance
- **KYC (Know Your Customer)**: Identity verification. Re-KYC = periodic document renewal required by Central Bank of Oman (CBO).
- **CBO (Central Bank of Oman)**: Regulatory authority. Governs fund holds, service availability, and customer protection.
- **WPS (Wage Protection System)**: Oman government mandatory salary payment system. Employers must pay salaries via WPS-approved channels. Registration requires the Sanad portal.
- **Sanad**: Oman Ministry of Manpower portal for WPS salary transfer registration. Branch staff cannot process WPS registrations directly — customers must use Sanad.
- **FATCA**: US tax compliance regulation affecting non-resident customers.

## Products & Schemes
- **Al Mazyona**: Bank Muscat prize draw savings scheme. Linked to savings accounts (OMR and AED). Regular prize draws for account holders.
- **Themaar**: Bank Muscat systematic savings plan (RO 10–1,000/month, 1–10 year tenure, includes life insurance).
- **Lulu Titanium Mastercard**: Co-branded credit card with Lulu Hypermarket. Earns 1% cashback + Lulu loyalty points on purchases.
- **bm wallet**: Bank Muscat digital mobile wallet for P2P transfers and merchant payments via QR code.
- **Speed Transfer**: Fast remittance service to India, Sri Lanka, Philippines, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Egypt. Nominal charges apply (RO 1.5 per transaction for most corridors).
- **Floosi Account**: Youth savings account for ages 13–17. Daily debit card limit: RO 50, monthly: RO 200.
- **Shababi Account**: Youth account for ages 18–22/23. Minimum balance RO 10.
- **Meethaq**: Bank Muscat's Islamic banking window. Products use Sharia-compliant structures (Murabaha, Ijarah, Musharaka). "Profit" replaces "interest"; "Financing" replaces "loan".
- **Kanzu**: Meethaq-branded debit/credit card.
- **Yaqdha**: Meethaq credit card product.

## Transfer Channels & SLAs
- **Within Bank Muscat**: Free, instant.
- **Other Bank Within Oman (< RO 3,000)**: RO 1.0 charge. Same day if submitted before 11:44 AM; next working day after.
- **Other Bank Within Oman (> RO 3,000)**: RO 4.250 charge. Same day if before 11:44 AM.
- **International SWIFT**: Non-Eastern currencies: same day before 14:00. Eastern currencies (AUD, SGD, JPY, NZD, HKD): next working day. SWIFT code: BKMOOMRX.
- **Speed Transfer India**: Same day per RBI guidelines. RO 1.5 charge.
