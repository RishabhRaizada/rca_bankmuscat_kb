# Enquiry (General Inquiries) — Knowledge Base

## Overview
This category covers informational customer inquiries that don't report a problem but seek information about Bank Muscat products, services, fees, or processes. The standard resolution for almost all enquiries is: **Contact Customer And Inform Accordingly**.

## Common Enquiry Types & What to Inform

### Loan / Finance Eligibility
- Personal loan/finance eligibility depends on: employment status, salary level, existing liabilities, credit history (CBO credit bureau).
- General requirement: Omani or expat resident with stable employment and salary transfer to Bank Muscat.
- Direct customer to nearest branch or call centre (24795555) for personalised eligibility assessment.

### SWIFT Charges / International Transfer Fees
- Bank Muscat charges for SWIFT transfers vary by destination and amount.
- mBanking indicative charges: RO 15 or RO 5 per SWIFT transaction (verify current rates on bankmuscat.com).
- Speed Transfer charges: RO 1.5 for most corridors; Egypt: free.
- Advise customer to check current fee schedule on bankmuscat.com or call 24795555.

### SWIFT Code
- Bank Muscat SWIFT/BIC code: **BKMOOMRX**
- Meethaq SWIFT code: **CFYCOMAAXXX**

### Countries Available for Fund Transfer
- SWIFT: Available to all countries connected to the SWIFT network globally.
- Speed Transfer: India, Sri Lanka, Philippines, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Egypt.

### Overseas Transfer Limit
- mBanking daily limit: RO 5,000 for international SWIFT (3 transactions/day).

### Account Statement
- Mini statement: Via ATM or mBanking (last 25 transactions).
- 3-month history: Via mBanking app.
- Extended/archived: Request at branch or internet banking; IT escalation for >3 months.

### Credit Card Requirements / Debit Card Requirements
- Debit card: Issued automatically with new accounts.
- Credit card: Requires application, credit assessment, and approval.
- Advise customer to visit branch or apply via mBanking.

### Current Account Requirements / Savings Account Requirements
- General requirements: Valid ID, residence proof, minimum initial deposit.
- Non-residents: Must visit branch; cannot open accounts online.
- Advise customer to visit nearest branch with original documents.

### Dormant Account Activation
- Accounts become dormant after a period of inactivity (typically 1–2 years).
- Activation: Customer must visit branch with valid ID and proof of residence.

### Fixed Deposit Cancellation Enquiry
- FDs can be cancelled (pre-mature withdrawal) at any time.
- Penalty: 1% of the applicable interest rate for the period held.
- Process: Written request at branch or contact call centre.

### Secure Token Device
- Issued at branch for internet banking two-factor authentication.
- Customer must activate after collection.
- Advise to visit nearest branch to collect or enquire about status.

### Increase Transaction Limit
- Transaction limits can be reviewed and increased upon request.
- Requires branch visit with supporting justification (business requirements, etc.).

### Airport Lounge Access / Airport Lounge Charges
- Select Bank Muscat premium cards include complimentary lounge access.
- Enquiries about specific card eligibility: check bankmuscat.com or call 24795555.
- Lounge access disputes: require Lounge Key Dispute Resolution Form.

### Card Activation
- New cards can be activated by generating PIN via mBanking (Requests → Debit/Credit Cards → Set/Reset PIN).
- Or call 24795555 for assisted activation.

### Credit Card PIN Reset
- Via mBanking: Requests → Credit Cards → Credit Card Set/Reset PIN.
- Requires OTP to mobile number.

### Create / Reset PIN for Debit Card
- Via mBanking: Requests → Debit Cards → Debit Card Set/Reset PIN.
- Or visit any Bank Muscat ATM.

### Balance Confirmation
- Formal balance confirmation letter: Request at branch. ARM (Account Relationship Manager) provides securities/investment-linked balance information.

### Opening VAT Invoice
- VAT invoices for Bank Muscat charges are sent by email or available through internet banking.
- If unable to open: Guide customer on how to login to internet banking; verify email address is correct; Rectify Technical Error if system issue.

### Speed Transfer Charges
- RO 1.5 for India, Sri Lanka, Philippines, Bangladesh, Pakistan; Free for Egypt.

### Special Rate Enquiry
- Special FD or transfer rates: Available for large deposits or premium customers. Advise to contact relationship manager or visit branch.

### Company Registration Requirements / Foreign Companies Account Requirements
- Business accounts require: Company registration (CR), board resolution, authorised signatory documents.
- Foreign companies: Additional requirements; advise to contact Corporate Banking team at branch.

### Lulu Points Enquiry
- Lulu Points earned via Lulu Titanium Mastercard. 1% cashback + Lulu loyalty points on purchases.
- Redemption: At Lulu Hypermarket locations.

## Key Operational Facts
- Enquiries are informational only — no corrective action is needed, only information provision.
- Root Cause for all enquiries: **Operational Issue** (information not readily accessible through self-service channels).
- Resolution for all enquiries: **Contact Customer And Inform Accordingly**.
- Many enquiries arise because information is not prominently published on bankmuscat.com or in the mobile app — a known content/UX gap.
