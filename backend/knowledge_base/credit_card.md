# Credit Card — Knowledge Base

## Overview
Bank Muscat issues Visa and Mastercard credit cards including co-branded products. This category covers credit card delivery, rewards, charges, activation, closure, and transfer requests. Key product: the Lulu Titanium Mastercard — Bank Muscat's co-branded card with Lulu Hypermarket, offering 1% cashback plus Lulu loyalty points.

## Products
- **Lulu Titanium Mastercard**: Co-branded with Lulu Hypermarket. Earns 1% cashback and Lulu loyalty points on purchases. This is Bank Muscat's flagship retail co-branded card.
- **Standard Credit Cards**: Visa and Mastercard in various tiers (Classic, Gold, Platinum, Titanium).
- **Meethaq Cards**: Kanzu and Yaqdha — Sharia-compliant credit cards for Meethaq Islamic banking customers.
- **Airport Lounge Access**: Some premium cards include lounge access. Disputes about lounge access require the "Lounge Key Dispute Resolution Form".

## Common Issues & Root Causes

### Card Not Received / Not Issued
- **What it is**: Customer applied for a card but hasn't received it. Card may be stuck in production, at Card Services, or in transit to branch.
- **Root Cause**: Branch Delay (card received but not notified), Operational Issue (logistics gap), Incomplete Documents (application not complete).
- **Resolution path**: Check with Card Services if card is printed → check with Mailing Department if card was sent to branch → if all clear, issue new card or escalate.

### Request to Transfer Card (Between Branches)
- **What it is**: Customer requested card delivery to a different branch than originally specified (e.g., from Sohar to Muscat Muttrah). Card may be in transit but no update given.
- **Root Cause**: Customer Request — legitimate service request. Operational breakdown is lack of tracking and communication.
- **Known pattern**: Inter-branch transfers lack real-time tracking. Customers repeatedly calling get only "it's with delivery" responses.
- **Resolution path**: Check With Card Services if card is printed → Check With Mailing Department to confirm card location → Contact The Customer with specific status and ETA.

### Card Not Activated
- **Root Cause**: Branch Delay (card issued but activation step missed), System Error (activation system failure), Operational Issue.
- **Resolution path**: Activate The Card directly; if system error, Escalate To IT; if card is damaged, Collect Destroyed Card From Customer and Issue New Card.

### Cash Back / Rewards Not Processed (Lulu Card)
- **What it is**: Customer's 1% cashback or Lulu points haven't been credited — often months delayed compared to previous years.
- **Root Cause**: Operational Issue — breakdown in automated rewards calculation/disbursement system or manual processing bottleneck.
- **Known pattern**: Cashback delays of 2+ months have been observed. Last credit was July 16th in one recorded case — indicating a systemic backlog.
- **Resolution path**: Conduct urgent audit of pending cashback disbursements; contact customer with resolution timeline; process backlog on priority basis.

### Wrong Charges
- **Root Cause**: Human Error (incorrect fee applied), System Error (automated charge calculated incorrectly), Operational Issue.
- **Resolution path**: Check customer account statement; rectify and refund if overcharged.

### Card Not Closed
- **Root Cause**: Back Office Delay, Branch Delay, System Error, or Customer Request (incomplete closure requirements).
- **Resolution path**: Check Additional Services Application; check T24 & Prime; contact customer for missing documents if needed.

## Internal Systems & Teams
- **Card Services**: Responsible for card production and readying for dispatch. First point of check when card is delayed.
- **Mailing Department**: Responsible for physical delivery of cards from Card Services to branches. Second point of check.
- **T24 & Prime**: Used to verify card status, billing, and transaction records.
- **Additional Services Application**: Used to process closure and service requests.

## SLAs & Key Facts
- Inter-branch card transfer: No published SLA, but delays beyond 1 week are complaint-worthy.
- Cashback: Should be credited monthly (1% of eligible purchases). Delays beyond 60 days are significant.
- Lounge key disputes: Require specific form ("Lounge Key Dispute Resolution Form").
- Card PIN can be instantly generated via mBanking app (Requests → Credit Cards → Credit Card Set/Reset PIN).
- Card blocking: Available instantly via mBanking app (Requests → Credit Cards → Block Credit Card).
