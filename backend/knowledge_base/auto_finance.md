# Auto Finance — Knowledge Base

## Overview
Bank Muscat provides vehicle financing (Auto Finance / Car Finance) for personal and commercial vehicles. Meethaq offers the Islamic equivalent via Ijarah (lease-to-own) or Murabaha structures. Auto Finance complaints are operationally similar to Home Finance but for vehicles rather than property.

## Common Issues & Root Causes

### Delay in Cancelling Finance/Loan Application
- **Root Cause**: Back Office Delay, Cancellation Fees Not Paid, Customer Retraction, Disbursement Completed, Incomplete Documents.
- **Resolution**: Contact The Customer → Request Customer For Missing Documents → Work In Progress → Rectify.

### Delay in Loan/Finance Processing (Amendment)
- **Root Cause**: Back Office Delay, Branch Delay, Incomplete Documents, Invalid Documents, Operational Issue.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer And Inform Accordingly → Escalation To Concerned Stakeholder → Rectify.

### Loan Settlement Issues
- **Root Cause**: Back Office Delay, Invalid Documents, Operational Issue.
- **Resolution**: Rectify → Work In Progress.

## Key Operational Facts
- Auto Finance settlement process mirrors Home Finance but without property documents (Mulkiya, Krooki).
- CAD (Credit Administration Department) handles settlement and rate administration.
- Meethaq Auto Finance uses "Profit" instead of "Interest", "Financing" instead of "Loan".
- Insurance is often a mandatory condition of Auto Finance — delays in insurance verification can block processing.
- Vehicle registration (Mulkiya for cars in Oman) may be held by bank as collateral until finance is fully settled.
