# Meethaq Islamic Banking — Knowledge Base

## Overview
Meethaq is Bank Muscat's dedicated Islamic banking window — the largest Islamic banking window in Oman. It operates as a division of Bank Muscat SAOG and uses Bank Muscat's full branch and ATM network (900+ access points). Meethaq is award-winning: "Best Islamic Bank in Oman" (Euromoney 2025) and "Pioneer in Digital Islamic Banking." All Meethaq products are Sharia-compliant, supervised by a 4-member Sharia Supervisory Board (SSB).

## Critical Terminology Difference
| Conventional Banking | Meethaq Islamic Banking |
|---|---|
| Loan | Financing |
| Interest | Profit |
| Interest rate | Profit rate |
| Loan settlement | Finance settlement |
| Interest-bearing | Profit-sharing |

In all RCA summaries for Meethaq complaints: use "Finance" not "Loan", "Profit" not "Interest".

## Products & Services

### Retail Banking
- **Current & Savings Accounts**: Sharia-compliant profit-sharing structures (Mudarabah).
- **Hibati Account**: Savings-plus account combining savings features and debit card access.
- **Personal Financing**: Unsecured and secured via Murabaha (cost-plus sale) or Ijarah (leasing).
- **Home Financing**: Islamic home ownership via Ijarah Muntahia Bittamleek (lease-to-own) or Murabaha.
- **Car Financing**: Via Ijarah (Islamic lease-purchase) or Murabaha.
- **Kanzu Card**: Meethaq-branded Sharia-compliant debit and credit card.
- **Yaqdha Credit Card**: Meethaq Sharia-compliant revolving credit facility.

### Corporate & SME Banking
- Trade finance: Islamic letters of credit, Murabaha-based import/export facilities.
- Working capital: Murabaha, Musharaka (profit-and-loss sharing), Ijarah.
- Corporate Online Banking (Corp-Online): Dedicated portal for Meethaq corporate customers.

### Investment
- **Meethaq Equity Fund**: Open-ended Islamic fund investing in Oman and GCC equities.
- **Sukuk**: Islamic capital market instruments (bonds structured as asset-backed securities).

### Digital Banking

## Common Meethaq-Specific Issues

### Meethaq Cheque Issues
- **Meethaq accounts have their own cheque books** (distinct from Bank Muscat standard cheques).
- Common issue: Special/Normal Cheque Book not received/issued.
- Root Cause: Human Error, Operational Issue, System Error.
- Resolution: Check Customer Details → Handover Cheque Book To Customer.

### Wrong Amount Posted (Meethaq Cheques)
- Root Cause: Customer Retraction or Human Error.
- Resolution: Check Customer Account Statement.

### Finance-Related Issues (Same as Home Finance, Auto Finance)
- Terminology: All "loan" references become "finance"; "interest" becomes "profit".
- Processes are identical to conventional banking (see Home Finance KB for details).

### Profit Rate Issues
- **Incorrect Rate / Profit Not Paid / Compound Profit Not Paid**
- Root Cause: Human Error, System Error.
- Resolution: Contact Customer; Rectify; recalculate correct profit and credit difference.

### Monthly Profit Not Added Back
- Root Cause: Human Error or System Error.
- Resolution: Contact Customer; Rectify in T24.

## Sharia Compliance Context
- **Murabaha**: Cost-plus sale. Bank purchases asset and sells to customer at agreed markup. Used for car and personal financing.
- **Ijarah**: Leasing. Bank owns asset, customer pays rental. Used for home financing.
- **Musharaka**: Partnership/profit-and-loss sharing. Used for business financing.
- **Ijarah Muntahia Bittamleek**: Lease ending in ownership. Used for home financing.
- The Sharia Supervisory Board (SSB) reviews all products. Compliance is non-negotiable.
- In customer communications, Sharia compliance should never be mentioned as a cause of delay — it is a structural feature, not an excuse.
