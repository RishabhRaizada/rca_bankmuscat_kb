# Current Account — Knowledge Base

## Overview
Bank Muscat current accounts are transactional accounts for individuals and businesses, available in OMR, USD, and EUR. They include cheque book facilities, debit card access, and Al Mazyona prize draw eligibility (OMR accounts). This category covers account transactions, fund blocks, unknown debits/credits, and account closure.

## Products
- **Standard Current Account**: Transactional account with cheque book and debit card. Available in OMR, USD, EUR.
- **Al Mazyona**: Prize draw savings scheme linked to current/savings accounts. Regular prize draws.
- **Cheque Book**: Issued on request; can also be requested via mBanking app.

## Common Issues & Root Causes

### Blocked Amount / Blocked Loan Installment
- **What it is**: Funds in the customer's account are blocked/held, reducing available balance without a clear explanation.
- **Root Cause**: Operational Issue (insurance or guarantee hold), Human Error (incorrect block applied), System Error (automated block triggered incorrectly).
- **Resolution path**: Check T24 → Check The Account → identify reason for block → Release The Locked Amount if unwarranted; Contact Customer with explanation.
- **Note**: Bancassurance-linked blocks require checking with Bancassurance Business Team. Loan installment blocks require verifying in T24 against loan schedule.

### Unknown Debit / Credit Transaction
- **What it is**: Customer sees a transaction they don't recognise on their statement.
- **Root Cause**: Human Error (wrong account debited), Operational Issue (charges applied incorrectly), System Error (duplicate or erroneous transaction).
- **Resolution**: Check Customer Account Statement → Contact Customer → Rectify if erroneous.

### Unknown Debit Transaction (Specific)
- **Resolution path**: Check The Customer Account And Provide The Necessary Details → Contact Customer → Rectify.

### Blocked Amount Not Released
- **Known pattern**: Insurance-linked blocks are a common type. Bancassurance Business Team must approve release.
- **Root Cause**: Branch Delay, Customer Request, Human Error, Operational Issue, or System Error.
- **Resolution path**: Branch Has To Check With Bancassurance Business Team (if insurance-related); Release The Locked Amount; Rectify Technical Error.

### Wrong Amount Posted / Wrong Amount Credited or Debited
- **Root Cause**: Human Error or System Error.
- **Resolution**: Check Customer Account Statement; Check Details And Rectify; Contact Customer.

### Account Not Opened
- **Root Cause**: Incomplete Documents, Invalid Documents, Operational Issue, Staff Delay, System Error.
- **Resolution**: Contact Customer For Missing Documents; Open Account; Rectify.
- **Note**: Online account opening is available via mBanking app for eligible customers. Non-residents, FATCA customers, or those with expired IDs must visit a branch.

### Account Not Closed
- **Root Cause**: Operational Issue (outstanding balance, linked credit card, active financing), Staff Delay.
- **Resolution path**: Call The Customer To Fill Application → Clear Balance (Contact Customer To Clear Outstanding Balance) → Close Account. Cannot close account with active credit card or outstanding financing.

### Account Opened With Wrong Details (Name, Currency, Phone, Email, ID, CR Number, Signature, Branch Entity)
- **Root Cause**: Human Error (data entry error at account opening), Branch Delay, Customer Request.
- **Resolution**: Check Customer AOF Form → Contact Customer → Modify The Account.
- **Note**: The AOF (Account Opening Form) is the original document — always check it first to determine if the error was the bank's or the customer's.

## Regulatory Context
- **Central Bank of Oman (CBO)**: Regulates fund holds and blocks. Unauthorised fund holds without proper authorisation or customer notification may violate CBO guidelines.
- Fund blocks must have proper authorisation and documentation. Dual authorisation should be required for holds above defined thresholds.

## Key Facts
- Account can be opened online via mBanking for eligible Omani residents with valid IDs.
- Non-residents, FATCA customers, expired IDs, un-updated KYC must visit branch.
- Al Mazyona accounts: OMR and AED only.
- Blocked amount release requires T24 check + back-office authorisation for significant holds.
